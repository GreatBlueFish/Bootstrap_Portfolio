About Me

Hello, my name is Ludwig, :) named after Beethoven and I home in Larsen Lake Bellevue, Washington. This is where my owner set me free and I have been taking care of the lake since! My hobbies include swimming, walking, eating insects, snails, worms and algae. Everyone asks if I'm related to the Ninja Turtles because I look like them, but I'm just a fan. I dream of one day being as great as Kanye West. I have a HUGE crush on Tom Brady and Rihanna is Bey. (;

If you haven't visited Larsen Lake I suggest you do because you're missing out. It's got the best scenery a turtle can ask for. Don't be that person, don't miss out! It's lonely here because I'm the only turtle, so feel free to visit anytime. You never know I might pop up and say Hello!
